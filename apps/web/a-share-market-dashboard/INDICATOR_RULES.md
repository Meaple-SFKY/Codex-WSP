# Indicator Rules

## Initial Indicators

- MA
- EMA
- MACD
- RSI
- KDJ
- Bollinger Bands
- Volume analysis

## Requirements

- Indicators must be deterministic.
- Indicator calculations should be isolated from UI.
- Indicator engines should be testable.
- Support multiple timeframes.

## Rendering

- Indicators should support overlay mode.
- Indicators should support separate-panel mode.

## Future Direction

Prepare for:

- custom indicators
- strategy indicators
- plugin-based indicators

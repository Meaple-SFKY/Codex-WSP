# Signal Analysis Rules

## Purpose

Signals are educational technical-analysis assistance only.

The app must not present signals as guaranteed investment advice.

## Initial Signal Types

- MACD crossover
- RSI overbought/oversold
- Volume breakout
- Trend continuation
- Trend reversal

## Requirements

- Every signal should explain why it was triggered.
- Every signal should include indicator references.
- Every signal should include confidence level.
- Every signal should include risk disclaimer.

## UX Rules

- Signals should be visually clear.
- Avoid excessive notification spam.
- Avoid false certainty language.
